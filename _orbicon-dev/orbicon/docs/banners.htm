Banners

Banners are added to the system through "Databases" menu. Depending on the format of the banner itself, it could be submitted either to Images or Documents database. Once the banner is added to the system we should flag it as a banner. Flagging the resource as a banner is simple and straightforward - click the checkbox next to your banner, from file actions menu select "Insert into banners" and confirm your action by clicking the button.

Banners are loaded into template using template mark <!>BANNER. (Multiple banners can be loaded this way)
