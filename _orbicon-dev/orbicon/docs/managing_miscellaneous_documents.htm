Managing miscellaneous documents

Open submenu "Documents" inside menu "Database". On the right sidebar are your document categories and on the left side is the list of those currently in the system. Adding documents follows the same pattern as uploading the images. Select the document and drag and drop it over the upload area in the right sidebar. After successful upload you can manually reload the page to view newly added documents.

Besided "drag and drop" method, images and documents can be added by using the "Browse" button.
