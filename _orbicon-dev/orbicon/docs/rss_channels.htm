RSS channels

The system will automatically generate RSS feeds for our news. However, we can also use feeds from other web sites and display them on our own.

This can be acomplished in two ways...

1. As part of news ticker

RSS channel becomes a part of content that is displayed when using template mark <!>NWS_TICKER

2. As a formatted box

RSS channel will be fromatted and displayed in place of its template mark (which system generates from RSS's URL).

For example, RSS channel http://rss.cnn.com/rss/cnn_topstories.rss will be given the following template mark <!>MYRSS_HTTP-RSS-CNN-COM-RSS-CNN_TOPSTORIES-RSS

This marks can be found under Dynamic elements » RSS
